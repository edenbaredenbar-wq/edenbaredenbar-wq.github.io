Our standard

We only recommend what we'd actually buy.

There's too much noise in the fitness and grooming space. Sponsored posts. Paid rankings. Affiliate farms that recommend whatever pays the highest commission. PeakForm isn't that — and it never will be.

Every pick on this site goes through a genuine research process: ingredients, clinical evidence, competitive testing, and honest value analysis. If something underdelivers, we say so. If a $20 product beats a $100 one, we say that too — even if the $100 product pays us more.

We earn through Amazon affiliate links on every product we list. That's the model. It's disclosed, it's transparent, and it costs you nothing extra. The affiliate structure is how we keep the lights on — but it doesn't decide what we pick. Our research process does.

How we pick

Four steps. Every category. No exceptions.

01

Market scan — We identify 30–50 products per category. Filtered by real review volume, recency, and verified purchase data. Paid placements are ignored entirely.

02

Ingredient check — Every active ingredient verified against clinical literature. No proprietary blends, no underdosed formulas, no marketing claims without evidence.

03

Value analysis — Cost per serving, cost per gram, cost per year. The best product isn't always cheapest — but it always has to justify its price clearly.

04

Final ranking — Scored on performance, value, availability, and longevity of the pick. The top product earns its place or the category stays empty.
