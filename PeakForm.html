// Our standard

We only recommend what we'd actually buy.

There's too much noise in the fitness and grooming space. Sponsored posts, paid rankings, affiliate farms that recommend anything with a high commission. PeakForm isn't that.

Every pick goes through a real research process — ingredients, reviews, competitive testing, and value for money. If something underdelivers, we say so. If a $20 product beats a $100 one, we say that too.

// How we pick

Four steps, every time

01

Market scan — We find the top 30–50 products in each category and filter by real customer review volume and recency, not paid placement.

02

Ingredient check — For supplements and skincare, every active ingredient is verified against clinical evidence. No proprietary blends hiding bad ratios.

03

Value analysis — Cost per gram, cost per serving, cost per year. The best product isn't always the cheapest — but it has to justify its price clearly.

04

Final ranking — We score on performance, value, availability, and longevity. The top pick earns its place or it doesn't get one.
